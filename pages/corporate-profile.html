Corporate Profile

The Institute of Personality Development and Customer Relationship Management (IPD-CRM) Nigeria [Member, International Council of Customer Service Orgainzation and World Personality Development Council] is a service oriented; professionally acknowledged regulatory body registered under the Copnaies and Allied Matters Act 1990. IPD-CRM is also duly registered with Corporate Affairs Commission and fully approved by the Federal ministry of Education and Federal Minstry of Justice, Nigeria respectively.

Operational Terms

  1. Personality Development:

The development of organized pattern of behaviors and attitudes that make a person distinctive. It is an enduring pattern or thoughts, fellings and behaviors that distinguishes individuals from one another. It is becoming a better person of yourself with Intergrity, Excellence and Professionalism as your collateral.

Personality development helps individuals to inspire others to follow him/her as a leader; to live with a positive attitude towards like and reducess stress ... Such a winning percweption helps individuals navigate through life;s possibilities and opportunities for personal, community, national and or global development.
