Associate Membership

Entry Requirements

Prospective Associate Members must be:

  1. Graduate of any Nigerian or foreign universities or higher education who has gone through the course: Poise & Customer Relationship for at least ONE semester in the final year. Eg. Successful final year student / graduates of Lead City University, Ibandan, earn the Associate Membership after graduation.
  2. Holders of B.Sc, B.A, B.Ed or HND from any recognized higher institution in or outside Niger, with not less than two (2) years work experience.
  3. Members of other professional bodies such as: NIPR, APCON, ICAN, CIBM, CIPM, NIMN, ACCA, NIM etc.
  4. Graduates from Nigerian or foreign Institutions.
  5. Numbers i, ii, iii, & iv must go through professional development workshop of the institute before Induction.

Full Membership

Entry Requirements

Prospective Full Members must fulfill the following:

  1. A graduate of Five (5) years and above.
  2. A manager or HOD or HOU with not less than Five (5) years of work experience.
  3. Must possess higher degrees (eg. M.Sc, MA, Ph.D) or be a member of relevant professional bodies.
  4. Politicians at the level of Commissioner, Local Government Chairman, Special Advisor, Party Chief, Member of State House of Assembly, etc.
  5. Must go through Professional Development Workshop of the institute before induction.
  6. The candidate must have spent four (4) years as an Associate Member.

Fellow Membership

Entry Requirements

This is the highest grade of membership.

To qualify for the Fellowship of the Institute, the person must fulfill the following:

  1. A graduate of Ten (10) years and above.
  2. Must have spent five (5) years as a Full member of IPD-CRM.
  3. Top Executives with experience: Chairman, MD, CEO / GM or Director with resposibility for managing Individuals or Corporate Relationshp at top management level.
